3. 1 Gene knock out 基因敲除技术基本原理
基因敲除（gene knock-out）又称基因打靶，通
过外源DNA与染色体DNA之间的同源重组，进行精
确的定点修饰和基因改造，具有专一性强、染色
体DNA可与目的片段共同稳定遗传等特点。


--- Page 18 ---
基因敲除分为完全基因敲除和条件型基因敲除
（又称不完全基因敲除）两种。
完全基因敲除是指通过同源重组法完全消除细胞
或者动物个体中的靶基因活性，条件型基因敲除
是指通过定位重组系统实现特定时间和空间的基
因敲除。

RNA Interference
• RNA interference occurs when a cell encounters 
dsRNA from a virus, transposon, or transgene 
• Trigger dsRNA is degraded into 21-25 nt fragments 
(siRNAs) by an RNase III-like enzyme called Dicer
• The guide strand of siRNA base-pairs with target 
mRNA in the active site of Ago2 in RISC (RNA-
induced silencing complex)
– Ago2 is an RNase H-like enzyme known as a slicer
– Slicer cleaves the target mRNA in the middle region 
of its base-pairing with the siRNA
– ATP-dependent step ejects the cleaved RNA from 
RISC which then accepts a new molecule of mRNA 
for degradation

Differences between siRNAs and miRNAs
•
siRNAs silence genes by degrading target mRNAs, while 
miRNAs interfere with translation mostly. However, miRNAs 
can also cause cleavage of target mRNAs that have a perfect 
match in sequence.---- (post-transcriptional gene silencing, or 
PTGS )
•
siRNAs are formed by Dicer action on double stranded RNAs,    
which could be exogenous or endogenous. In contrast, 
miRNAs are formed by sequential action by Drosha and Dicer 
on the double-stranded part of a stem-loop RNA that is a 
normal cellular product.
•
siRNAs base-pair perfectly with target mRNAs, whereas 
miRNAs usually base-pair imperfectly with their target 
mRNAs.
•
RNAi can be involved in histone methylation, and the resulting 
heterochromatin formation and gene silencing.----- 
(transcriptional gene silencing, or TGS)

In vivo protein-protein interaction
FRET荧光能量转移有三个基本条件：
（1）给体与受体在合适的距离（1～10 nm）；
（2）给体的发射光谱与受体的吸收光谱有一定的重叠（这是能量
匹配的条件）；
（3）给体与受体的偶极具有一定的空间取向（这是偶极-偶极耦合
作用的条件）。
