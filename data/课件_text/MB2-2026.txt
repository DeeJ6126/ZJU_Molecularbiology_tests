•
DNA denaturation (melting): the doubled strands of DNA completely 
separated each other.
•
Nucleic acids absorb UV light at 260nm because of the electronic 
structure in their bases, but when two strands of DNA come together, 
the close proximity of the bases in the two strands quenches some of 
this absorbance. When the two DNA strands separate, this quenching 
disappears and the absorbance rises 30-40%.
• Tm:  the temperature at which the shift in absorbance is half completed.
 
•
More GC, higher Tm
Physical Chemistry of Nucleic Acids
