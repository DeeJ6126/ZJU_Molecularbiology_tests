Mendel’s laws of inheritance
• Every cell contained pairs of factors and that each pair determined a 
specific trait.  One allele can be dominant over another.
• Principle of segregation (分离规律): The members of each pair segregated 
from each other in the process of sex-cell formation, so that a gamete 
contained one member of each pair.遗传因子在体细胞内是成对存在的，
一个来自父本，一个来自母本，只有在形成配子时单独存在。
• Principle of independent assortment (独立分配规律): The segregation of 
each pair was independent of the segregation of other pairs of factors.各性
状成对的遗传因子在分配给配子体的分离过程中彼此是独立的。

The Hershey—Chase experiment (1952) 
Phage T2 contains genes that allow it to replicate in E. coli. Because the phage is 
composed of DNA and protein only, its genes must be made of one of these substances. 
To discover which, Hershey and Chase performed a two-part experiment. In the first part 
(a), they labeled the phage protein with 35S (red), leaving the DNA unlabeled (black). In 
the second part (b), they labeled the phage DNA with 32P (red), leaving the protein 
unlabeled (black). Since the phage genes must enter the cell, the experimenters reasoned 
that the type of label found in the infected cells would indicate the nature of the genes. 
Most of the labeled protein remained on the outside and was stripped off the cells by use 
of a blender (a), whereas most of the labeled DNA entered the infected cells (b). 
Conclusion : the genes of this phage are made of DNA.
T2噬菌体带有能使其在大肠杆菌中复制的基因。由于噬菌体内只含DNA和蛋白质，
二者中必有其一是携带有其遗传信息的基因。为了发现哪一个成份是它的遗传物
质， Hershey和Chase 作了如下二个实验。第一个实验中，他们用35S标记了噬菌
体的蛋白质，而DNA没有用放射性元素标记。第二个实验中，他们用32P标记了噬
菌体的DNA，而蛋白质没有被标记。由于噬菌体的基因必定要进入细菌细胞中，可
根据受感染的细胞中发现的放射性物质的种类推断什么是噬菌体的遗传物质。实
验结果表明，绝大多数的标记蛋白留在细菌的胞外，可用匀浆器将其洗脱（图a）；
而绝大多数的标记DNA进入到被感染的细胞中（ 图b）。因此实验的结论是，DNA
是噬菌体的遗传物质。
